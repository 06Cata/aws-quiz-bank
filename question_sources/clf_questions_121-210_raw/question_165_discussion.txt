mkhwezi 1 year, 11 months ago
D. IAM Access Analyzer
IAM Access Analyzer helps you identify resources in your AWS account, such as S3 buckets, that are shared with external principals (including other
AWS accounts). It continuously monitors resource policies for public and cross-account access to identify any unintended or risky configurations.
