Selected Answer: A
The correct answer is:
A. Management of the guest operating systems.
Explanation:
Under the AWS shared responsibility model, AWS is responsible for the security of the cloud (the infrastructure, hardware, and software that supports
AWS services), while the customer is responsible for security in the cloud (the configuration and security of their own applications, data, and services)
The customer is responsible for managing their guest operating systems, which are the operating systems they install and manage on their EC2
instances. This includes ensuring the operating system is updated, patched, and secured.
