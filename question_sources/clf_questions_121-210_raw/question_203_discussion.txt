The AWS Partner Network (APN) is designed to help companies find qualified third-party consultants, software vendors, and managed service
providers who are specialized in working with AWS services. APN Partners offer various levels of support and expertise to assist with maintaining and
optimizing AWS environments based on specific business needs.
