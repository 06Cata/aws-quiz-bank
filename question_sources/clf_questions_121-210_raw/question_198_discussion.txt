Selected Answer: B
"D. Maintain the physical hardware of the infrastructure" is not the best answer because, according to the AWS shared responsibility model, AWS is
responsible for managing the physical infrastructure of the cloud, including data centers and hardware maintenance. However, it's the customer's
responsibility to secure and manage the data and applications they deploy on AWS infrastructure. Therefore, option B is the best choice as it aligns
with AWS's responsibility for managing encryption options for data stored on their platform.
