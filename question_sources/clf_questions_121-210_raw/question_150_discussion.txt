Availability Zones
An Availability Zone (AZ) is one or more discrete data centers with redundant power, networking, and connectivity in an AWS Region. AZs give
customers the ability to operate production applications and databases that are more highly available, fault tolerant, and scalable than would be
possible from a single data center.
