Selected Answer: D
A: Amazon EventBridge is used for building event-driven applications by connecting services with events, not for checking AWS service status or
availability.
B: AWS Service Catalog helps manage and deploy approved products and services within an organization but doesn’t provide service availability info
C: AWS Control Tower is used for setting up and governing multi-account AWS environments, not for monitoring service health.
D: AWS Health Dashboard provides personalized alerts and real-time information about AWS service availability and operations that affect your
account.
