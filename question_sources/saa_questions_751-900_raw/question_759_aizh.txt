demand for movies that are less than 20 years old than for movies that are more than 20 years old. The company wants to
minimize hosting service costs based on demand.

A. Store all media content in Amazon S3. Use S3 Lifecycle policies to move media data into the Infrequent Access tier
when the demand for a movie decreases.

B. Store newer movie video files in S3 Standard. Store older movie video files in S3 Standard-infrequent Access (S3
Standard-IA). When a user orders an older movie, retrieve the video file by using standard retrieval.
桭畧性分析：S3 Standard适合存储高需梆数据，S3 Standard-IA适合存储访问频栞较低但仍需要快速桌痋甹数据。桮选项明畧区分

桭畧性分析：S3 Intelligent-Tiering可以动态调整存储层痔，适合较新甹甤影。S3 Glacier Flexible Retrieval适合存储长期归桂数据，
