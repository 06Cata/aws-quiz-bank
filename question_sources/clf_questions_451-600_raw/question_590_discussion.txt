Selected Answer: B
AWS Fargate is a serverless compute engine for containers. Fargate is a container hosting platform, unlike Amazon ECS and Amazon EKS, which are
both container orchestration services.
When using Fargate, you do not need to provision or manage servers. Fargate manages your server infrastructure for you. You can focus more on
innovating and developing your applications, and you pay only for the resources that are required to run your containers.
