Selected Answer: C
S3 Glacier Vault Lock provides the ability to lock your Glacier vaults to enforce WORM protection. Once a vault is locked with a retention policy, data
cannot be modified or deleted for the specified retention period, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements for write-once, read-many
storage. This feature is specifically designed to meet compliance and regulatory requirements for immutable data storage.
