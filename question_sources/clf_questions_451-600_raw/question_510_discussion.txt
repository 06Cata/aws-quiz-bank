Economies of scale is a key benefit of the AWS Cloud that allows companies to achieve lower costs due to the vast aggregate usage across all AWS
customers. AWS can buy and operate infrastructure at a massive scale, which reduces per-unit costs and allows AWS to pass these savings on to
customers. This leads to lower prices for cloud services compared to what individual companies might pay for their own infrastructure.
