Savings Plans offer significant savings compared to On-Demand pricing, with flexibility to change instance types, operating systems, and regions.
Given the predictable and long-term nature of the workload, a 3-year commitment to Savings Plans will provide the most cost-effective option
while ensuring the required compute resources are always available.
