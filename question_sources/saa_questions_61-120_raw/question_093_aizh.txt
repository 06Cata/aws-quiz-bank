A company runs an on-premises application that is powered by a MySQL database. The company is migrating the application to
AWS to increase the application's elasticity and availability.
The current architecture shows heavy read activity on the database during times of normal operation. Every 4 hours, the
company's development team pulls a full export of the production database to populate a database in the staging environment.
During this period, users experience unacceptable application latency. The development team is unable to use the staging
environment until the procedure completes.
A solutions architect must recommend replacement architecture that alleviates the application latency issue. The replacement
architecture also must give the development team the ability to continue using the staging environment without delay.
Which solution meets these requirements?
