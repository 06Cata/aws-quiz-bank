The correct answer is:
A. Data architecture
The Platform Perspective of the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (AWS CAF) focuses on the capabilities required to design and implement a cloud-
based platform. Data architecture is a key capability under this perspective, as it involves designing the structure and organization of data systems to
support business needs in the cloud.
The other options are not part of the Platform Perspective:
B. Data protection: Falls under the Security Perspective.
