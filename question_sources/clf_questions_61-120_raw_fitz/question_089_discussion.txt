Question #89
Which of the following are advantages of moving to the AWS Cloud? (Choose two.)
A. The ability to turn over the responsibility for all security to AWS.
B. The ability to use the pay-as-you-go model. Most Voted
C. The ability to have full control over the physical infrastructure.
D. No longer having to guess what capacity will be required. Most Voted
E. No longer worrying about users access controls.

B. The ability to use the pay-as-you-go model - AWS provides a flexible and cost-effective pay-as-you-go pricing model, allowing users to pay only
