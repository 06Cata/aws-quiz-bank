Consolidated billing has the following benefits:
- ONE BILL – You get one bill for multiple accounts.
- Combined usage – You can combine the usage across all accounts in the organization to share the VOLUME PRICING DISCOUNTS, Reserved
Instance discounts, and Savings Plans. This can result in a lower charge for your project, department, or company than with individual standalone
accounts.

The benefits of consolidated billing for AWS Cloud services are:
C. One bill for multiple accounts: Consolidated billing allows you to receive a single bill for multiple AWS accounts, making it easier to manage and
track costs across all accounts. This simplifies the billing process and provides a unified view of spending.
A. Volume discounts: Consolidated billing enables you to aggregate the usage of multiple accounts, which may qualify you for volume discounts

accounts may not be eligible for.
