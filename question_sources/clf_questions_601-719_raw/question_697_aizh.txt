A company purchased Amazon EC2 Standard Reserved Instances (RIs) for a workload in the AWS Cloud. The company needs to
move part of the workload to an instance family that does not match the instance family of these Standard RIs.
How can the company take advantage of the Standard RIs that it no longer needs?
一家公司为其在AWS云中的工作负载购买了Amazon EC2标准预留实例（RIs）。该公司需要将部分工作负载迁移到一个与这些标准
预留实例的实例系列不匹配的实例系列。
公司如何利用它不再需要的标准预留实例？
A. Contact the AWS Support team, and ask the team to sell the Standard RIs
联系AWS支持团队，并请求团队出售标准预留实例
B. Sell the Standard RIs on the Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Marketplace
在Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Marketplace上出售标准预留实例
C. Sell the Standard RIs as a third-party seller on the AWS Marketplace
作为第三方卖家在AWS Marketplace上出售标准预留实例
D. Convert the Standard RIs to Savings Plans
将标准预留实例转换为Savings Plans
题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
本题考察的是AWS EC2 Standard Reserved Instances (RIs)的使用和管理，包括如何处理不再需要的标准预留实例。
涉及的知识点包括Reserved Instance Marketplace的功能和限制。
2. 选项分析
Correct Answer: B
正确答案：B
Community vote distribution
社区投票分布
B (100%)
A. Contact the AWS Support team, and ask the team to sell the Standard RIs
错误：AWS Support团队无法直接帮助客户出售标准预留实例。客户需要自己在Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Marketplace上进行
操作。
B. Sell the Standard RIs on the Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Marketplace
正确：Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Marketplace允许客户出售不再需要的标准预留实例给其他AWS用户。
C. Sell the Standard RIs as a third-party seller on the AWS Marketplace
错误：AWS Marketplace是用于销售软件和服务的市场，而不是用于出售EC2预留实例。
D. Convert the Standard RIs to Savings Plans
错误：标准预留实例无法直接转换为Savings Plans。Savings Plans是一种不同的定价模型，与预留实例无直接转换关系。
3. 我的答案
B. Sell the Standard RIs on the Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Marketplace
