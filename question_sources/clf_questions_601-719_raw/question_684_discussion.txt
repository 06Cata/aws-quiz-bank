Selected Answer: C
There is no time specified, so you cannot assume that the application will have to run for a year, which makes an on-demand more logical.
• On-demand: Like a resort where you come and pay full price whenever you like.
• Reserved: You plan ahead and commit to staying for one to three years, receiving a discount.
• Savings plan: You commit to spending a specific amount monthly, allowing flexibility in room types.
• Spot instances: Last-minute discounts for empty rooms, but you can be asked to leave if someone pays more.
• Dedicated host: You book the entire resort building, having your own hardware.
Capacity reservation: You book a room without certainty of staying, paying full price regardless.
