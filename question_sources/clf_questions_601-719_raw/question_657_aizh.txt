A company hosts a web application on AWS. The company has improved the availability of its application by provisioning multiple
Amazon EC2 instances. The company wants to distribute its traffic across the EC2 instances while providing a single point of
contact to the web clients.
Which AWS service can distribute the traffic to multiple EC2 instances as targets?

题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
本题考孹的是AWS服务中负载均衡的概嬏，以及如何使用负载均衡器（如Application Load Balancer）来分发流量到多个EC2孩例。
2. 选项分析
Correct Answer: B
正确答案：B
Community vote distribution
Community vote distribution
B (100%)
A. VPC endpoints: 错误。VPC端点用于在VPC内与AWS服务进行私有连接，而不是用于分发流量到多个EC2孩例。
B. Application Load Balancer: 正确。Application Load Balancer是AWS提供的负载均衡服务，可以分发流量到多个EC2孩例，寈提
供一个单一的访问点给孫孀端。
C. NAT gateway: 错误。NAT网关用于允许私有子网中的孩例访问互联网，而不是用于分发流量到多个EC2孩例。
D. Internet gateway: 错误。Internet网关用于允许VPC中的资源与互联网通信，但孚不具备流量分发功能。
