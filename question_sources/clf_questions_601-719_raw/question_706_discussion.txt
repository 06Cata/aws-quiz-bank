To meet the company’s need to monitor the ongoing costs of the e-commerce website and keep expenses within a specific budget, the most suitable
AWS service is AWS Cost Explorer. Cost Explorer provides detailed cost analysis, budget monitoring, and forecasting capabilities, allowing the
company to track the website’s operational costs (e.g., S3 storage for product images, EC2 compute costs) and ensure they don’t exceed the budget.
Final Answer: A. AWS Cost Explorer.
