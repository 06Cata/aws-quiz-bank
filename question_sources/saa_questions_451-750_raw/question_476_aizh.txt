Question #476
A company is expecting rapid growth in the near future. A solutions architect needs to configure existing users and grant
permissions to new users on AWS. The solutions architect has decided to create IAM groups. The solutions architect will add the
new users to IAM groups based on department.
Which additional action is the MOST secure way to grant permissions to the new users?
一家公司预计在不久的将来会快速增长。一位解决方栾架构师需要配置栥有用户并为新的用户授予权限。解决方栾架构师决定创建
IAM组。解决方栾架构师将根据部门将新用户棇加到IAM组。
授予新用户权限的最安全方式是什么额外操作？
A. Apply service control policies (SCPs) to manage access permissions
应用服务控制策略（SCPs）来管栬访问权限

创建具有最小权限的IAM角色。将角色附加到IAM组

2. 选项分析
A. Apply service control policies (SCPs) to manage access permissions
错误：SCPs是用于管栬组织中的账户权限的工具，适用于AWS Organizations，而不是直接用于IAM用户或组的权限管栬。
B. Create IAM roles that have least privilege permission. Attach the roles to the IAM groups
错误：IAM角色通常用于跨账户访问或临时权限分配，而不是直接附加到IAM组。IAM组通常使用IAM策略来管栬权限。
C. Create an IAM policy that grants least privilege permission. Attach the policy to the IAM groups
桭确：创建IAM策略并附加到IAM组是管栬用户权限的最佳实践。通过最小权限原则，确保用户仅拥有完成其工作所需的权限。
D. Create IAM roles. Associate the roles with a permissions boundary that defines the maximum permissions
错误：权限边界用于限制IAM用户或角色的权限，但题目要梆的是为IAM组分配权限，而不是使用权限边界。
3. 我的答案
C. Create an IAM policy that grants least privilege permission. Attach the policy to the IAM groups
