Question #585
A solutions architect is designing a disaster recovery (DR) strategy to provide Amazon EC2 capacity in a failover AWS Region.
Business requirements state that the DR strategy must meet capacity in the failover Region.
Which solution will meet these requirements?
解决方案架构师正在设计一个灾难恢复 (DR) 策略，以在故障转移的 AWS 区域中提供 Amazon EC2 容量。业务需求规定 DR 策略必
须满足故障转移区域的容量要求。
哪种解决方案可以满足这些需求？
A. Purchase On-Demand Instances in the failover Region.
在故障转移区域购买 On-Demand Instances。
B. Purchase an EC2 Savings Plan in the failover Region.
在故障转移区域购买 EC2 Savings Plan。
C. Purchase regional Reserved Instances in the failover Region.
在故障转移区域购买区域 Reserved Instances。
D. Purchase a Capacity Reservation in the failover Region. Most Voted
在故障转移区域购买 Capacity Reservation。
题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
本题考察的是AWS EC2容量管理和灾难恢复（Disaster Recovery）策略，特别是如何确保在故障转移区域（Failover Region）能够满
足业务需求的计算容量。
Correct Answer: D
正确答案：D

2. 选项分析
A. Purchase On-Demand Instances in the failover Region.
错误：On-Demand Instances是按需购买的实例，虽然可以快速启动，但无法保证在故障转移时有足够的容量可用，因为容量可能
会受到其他用户的竞争影响。
B. Purchase an EC2 Savings Plan in the failover Region.
错误：EC2 Savings Plan主要用于优化成本，而不是保证容量。它无法确保在故障转移区域有足够的计算资源可用。
C. Purchase regional Reserved Instances in the failover Region.
错误：虽然Reserved Instances可以提供成本优化和容量预留，但它的容量预留是基于实例类型和区域的长期承诺，无法灵活地满足
灾难恢复的需求。
D. Purchase a Capacity Reservation in the failover Region.
正确：Capacity Reservation允许您在指定的区域和可用区中预留EC2容量，确保在故障转移时有足够的计算资源可用。这是灾难恢
复场景中最合适的选项。
3. 我的答案
D. Purchase a Capacity Reservation in the failover Region.
4. 官方答案
D. Purchase a Capacity Reservation in the failover Region.
