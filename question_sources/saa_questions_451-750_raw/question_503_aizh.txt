Question #503
A company runs an infrastructure monitoring service. The company is building a new feature that will enable the service to
monitor data in customer AWS accounts. The new feature will call AWS APIs in customer accounts to describe Amazon EC2
instances and read Amazon CloudWatch metrics.
What should the company do to obtain access to customer accounts in the MOST secure way?
一家公司运行一个基础设施监控服务。该公司正在开发一个新功能，该功能将使服务能够监控客户AWS账户中的数据。新功能将调
用客户账户中的AWS APIs，以描述Amazon EC2实例并读取Amazon CloudWatch指标。
公司应该如何以最安全的方式获得客户账户的访问权限？
A. Ensure that the customers create an IAM role in their account with read-only EC2 and CloudWatch permissions and a trust

题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
IAM角色与权限管理
跨账户访问的最佳实践
安全性与信任策略的配置
2. 选项分析
A. Ensure that the customers create an IAM role in their account with read-only EC2 and CloudWatch permissions and a
trust policy to the company’s account.
正确性分析：这是AWS推荐的跨账户访问最佳实践。通过IAM角色，客户可以授予公司账户权限，同时通过信任策略确保只有公司
账户可以访问该角色。
安全性：IAM角色使用临时凭证，避免了长期凭证泄露的风险。
结论：正确。
B. Create a serverless API that implements a token vending machine to provide temporary AWS credentials for a role with
read-only EC2 and CloudWatch permissions.
正确性分析：虽然可以通过令牌分发机制提供临时凭证，但这种方法复杂且不符合AWS跨账户访问的最佳实践。
安全性：增加了额外的管理复杂性，可能引入安全风险。
结论：错误。
C. Ensure that the customers create an IAM user in their account with read-only EC2 and CloudWatch permissions. Encrypt
and store customer access and secret keys in a secrets management system.
正确性分析：使用IAM用户和长期凭证进行跨账户访问不符合AWS最佳实践。
安全性：长期凭证存在泄露风险，即使加密存储也无法完全避免。
结论：错误。
D. Ensure that the customers create an Amazon Cognito user in their account to use an IAM role with read-only EC2 and
CloudWatch permissions. Encrypt and store the Amazon Cognito user and password in a secrets management system.
正确性分析：Amazon Cognito主要用于身份验证和授权，与跨账户访问无关。
安全性：存储用户密码增加了管理复杂性和安全风险。
结论：错误。
