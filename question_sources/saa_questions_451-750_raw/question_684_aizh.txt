Question #684
A company wants to migrate its web applications from on premises to AWS. The company is located close to the eu-central-1
Region. Because of regulations, the company cannot launch some of its applications in eu-central-1. The company wants to
achieve single-digit millisecond latency.
Which solution will meet these requirements?
一家公司希望将其网页应用程序从本地迁移到AWS。该公司位于靠近eu-central-1区域的地方。由于法规限制，该公司无法在eu-
central-1区域启动其部分应用程序。该公司希望实现个位数毫秒级延迟。
哪种解决方案可以满足这些要求？
A. Deploy the applications in eu-central-1. Extend the company’s VPC from eu-central-1 to an edge location in Amazon
CloudFront.
将应用程序部署在eu-central-1区域。通过将公司的VPC从eu-central-1扩展到Amazon CloudFront的边缘位置。
B. Deploy the applications in AWS Local Zones by extending the company's VPC from eu-central-1 to the chosen Local Zone.

题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
了解AWS Local Zones的功能和使用场景。
理解如何实现低延迟架构设计。
熟悉AWS服务（如CloudFront、Local Zones、Wavelength Zones）的特点和适用场景。
2. 选项分析
A. Deploy the applications in eu-central-1. Extend the company’s VPC from eu-central-1 to an edge location in Amazon
CloudFront.
错误：CloudFront的边缘位置主要用于内容分发（CDN），而不是用于托管应用程序。它无法满足单数字毫秒延迟的要求。
B. Deploy the applications in AWS Local Zones by extending the company's VPC from eu-central-1 to the chosen Local
Zone.
正确：AWS Local Zones允许用户在靠近最终用户的区域运行应用程序，从而实现低延迟（单数字毫秒）。这符合题目要求。
C. Deploy the applications in eu-central-1. Extend the company’s VPC from eu-central-1 to the regional edge caches in
Amazon CloudFront.
错误：CloudFront的区域边缘缓存主要用于优化内容分发的性能，而不是托管应用程序。它无法满足单数字毫秒延迟的要求。
D. Deploy the applications in AWS Wavelength Zones by extending the company’s VPC from eu-central-1 to the chosen
Wavelength Zone.
错误：AWS Wavelength Zones主要用于5G网络的边缘计算场景，适用于移动设备的低延迟需求。题目未提到5G或移动设备相关的
需求，因此不适合此场景。
3. 我选的答案
B

求。
