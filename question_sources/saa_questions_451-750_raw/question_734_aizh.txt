Question #734
A company has an AWS Direct Connect connection from its corporate data center to its VPC in the us-east-1 Region. The company
recently acquired a corporation that has several VPCs and a Direct Connect connection between its on-premises data center and
the eu-west-2 Region. The CIDR blocks for the VPCs of the company and the corporation do not overlap. The company requires
connectivity between two Regions and the data centers. The company needs a solution that is scalable while reducing operational
overhead.
What should a solutions architect do to meet these requirements?

业通过Direct Connect连接其本地数据中心与eu-west-2区域。 该公司的VPC和被收购企业的VPC的CIDR块没有重叠。 该公司需要在
两个区域和数据中心之间实现连接。 该公司需要一个可扩展的解决方案，同时减少运营开销。
解决方案架构师应该怎么做来满足这些需求？
A. Set up inter-Region VPC peering between the VPC in us-east-1 and the VPCs in eu-west-2.
在us-east-1区域的VPC与eu-west-2区域的VPC之间设置跨区域VPC对等连接。

题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
跨区域网络连接（Inter-Region Networking）
AWS Direct Connect的使用和Direct Connect Gateway的功能
VPC网络架构设计与优化
减少运营开销和实现可扩展性
2. 选项分析
A. Set up inter-Region VPC peering between the VPC in us-east-1 and the VPCs in eu-west-2.
正确性分析：跨区域VPC对等连接可以实现VPC之间的通信，但它需要手动设置每个对等连接，且不支持直接连接到本地数据中
心。这种方法增加了运营开销，不符合题目要求。
结论：错误。
B. Create private virtual interfaces from the Direct Connect connection in us-east-1 to the VPCs in eu-west-2.
正确性分析：Direct Connect的私有虚拟接口只能连接到同一区域的VPC，无法直接跨区域连接到其他区域的VPC。因此，这种方法
不可行。
结论：错误。
C. Establish VPN appliances in a fully meshed VPN network hosted by Amazon EC2. Use AWS VPN CloudHub to send and
receive data between the data centers and each VPC.
正确性分析：使用VPN CloudHub可以实现跨区域和数据中心的连接，但需要设置和维护EC2实例作为VPN设备。这种方法增加了运
营开销，不符合题目要求的“减少运营开销”。
结论：错误。
D. Connect the existing Direct Connect connection to a Direct Connect gateway. Route traffic from the virtual private
gateways of the VPCs in each Region to the Direct Connect gateway.
正确性分析：Direct Connect Gateway支持跨区域连接，可以将多个区域的VPC连接到本地数据中心，同时减少运营开销并提供可扩
展性。这种方法完全符合题目要求。
结论：正确。
