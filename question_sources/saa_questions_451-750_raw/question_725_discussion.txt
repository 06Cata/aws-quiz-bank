A company maintains about 300 TB in Amazon S3 Standard storage month after month. The S3 objects are each typically around
50 GB in size and are frequently replaced with multipart uploads by their global application. The number and size of S3 objects
remain constant, but the company's S3 storage costs are increasing each month.
How should a solutions architect reduce costs in this situation?
A. Switch from multipart uploads to Amazon S3 Transfer Acceleration.
B. Enable an S3 Lifecycle policy that deletes incomplete multipart uploads. Most Voted
C. Configure S3 inventory to prevent objects from being archived too quickly.
D. Configure Amazon CloudFront to reduce the number of objects stored in Amazon S3.
