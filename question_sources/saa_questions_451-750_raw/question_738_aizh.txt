Question #738
A company has a new mobile app. Anywhere in the world, users can see local news on topics they choose. Users also can post
photos and videos from inside the app.
Users access content often in the first minutes after the content is posted. New content quickly replaces older content, and then
the older content disappears. The local nature of the news means that users consume 90% of the content within the AWS Region
where it is uploaded.
Which solution will optimize the user experience by providing the LOWEST latency for content uploads?
一家公司推出了一款新的移动应用程序。在世界任何地方，用户都可以看到他们选择的主题的本地新闻。用户还可以在应用程序内
发布照片和视频。
用户通常会在内容发布后的几分钟内访问内容。新内容会迅速替代旧内容，然后旧内容会消失。新闻的本地化特性意味着用户会在
内容上传所在的AWS Region内消费90%的内容。
哪种解决方案可以通过提供昀低的上传延迟来优化用户体验？
A. Upload and store content in Amazon S3. Use Amazon CloudFront for the uploads.
将内容上传并存储到Amazon S3。使用Amazon CloudFront进行上传。

题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
Amazon S3的存储特性
S3 Transfer Acceleration的功能与优势
Amazon CloudFront的内容分发特性
如何优化内容上传的低延迟和高效率
2. 选项分析
A. Upload and store content in Amazon S3. Use Amazon CloudFront for the uploads.
错误：Amazon CloudFront主要用于内容分发（下载），而不是优化上传。使用CloudFront进行上传并不能显著降低延迟。
B. Upload and store content in Amazon S3. Use S3 Transfer Acceleration for the uploads.
正确：S3 Transfer Acceleration通过使用AWS的全球加速网络优化上传速度，尤其适合跨区域上传内容。
C. Upload content to Amazon EC2 instances in the Region that is closest to the user. Copy the data to Amazon S3.
错误：虽然EC2实例可以提供低延迟，但需要额外的管理和维护工作。将数据从EC2复制到S3增加了复杂性和成本。
D. Upload and store content in Amazon S3 in the Region that is closest to the user. Use multiple distributions of Amazon
CloudFront.
错误：虽然将内容存储在昀接近用户的Region可以降低延迟，但CloudFront的主要功能是优化内容分发（下载），而不是上传。
3. 我选的答案
B. Upload and store content in Amazon S3. Use S3 Transfer Acceleration for the uploads.
4. 官方答案
B. Upload and store content in Amazon S3. Use S3 Transfer Acceleration for the uploads.
5. 与官方答案比较
我的答案与官方答案一致。
原因：S3 Transfer Acceleration是专门设计用于优化跨区域上传的工具，能够显著降低延迟并提高上传速度。
