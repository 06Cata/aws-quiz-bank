Selected Answer: D
D. Reserved Instances
Reserved Instances offer significant cost savings compared to On-Demand Instances, especially for workloads that are expected to run continuously
for a long duration, such as the next 12 months in this scenario. By committing to a 1- or 3-year term with Reserved Instances, the company can
benefit from discounted hourly rates compared to On-Demand pricing.
