Question #294
Which Amazon S3 storage class is MOST cost-effective for unknown access patterns?
A. S3 Standard
B. S3 Standard-Infrequent Access (S3 Standard-IA)
C. S3 One Zone-Infrequent Access (S3 One Zone-IA)
D. S3 Intelligent-Tiering Most Voted
Comments
Mike1987 Highly Voted 2 years, 6 months ago
Selected Answer: D
D. S3 Intelligent-Tiering
S3 Intelligent-Tiering is the most cost-effective option for unknown access patterns. It automatically moves objects between two access tiers
(frequent and infrequent access) based on changing access patterns. This helps optimize costs for data with unknown or changing access patterns.

Selected Answer: D
A: S3 Standard – Good for frequent access, but not optimized for cost when access patterns are unknown or infrequent.
B: S3 Standard-IA – Lower cost for infrequent access, but incurs extra charges if data is accessed more than expected.
C: S3 One Zone-IA – Cheaper but stores data in one Availability Zone, making it less durable and not ideal for unpredictable patterns.
D: S3 Intelligent-Tiering , it's designed specifically for unknown or changing access patterns. It automatically moves data between frequent and
infrequent access tiers based on usage, optimizing cost without performance loss.
