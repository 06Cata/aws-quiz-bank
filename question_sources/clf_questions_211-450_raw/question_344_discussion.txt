Governance in AWS CAF involves establishing policies, controls, and mechanisms to ensure that the organization's use of the cloud aligns with

Identity and Access Management (IAM)
Cloud Financial Management
Security
Compliance
Operations
Resource Lifecycle Management
Risk Management
Change Management
Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)
