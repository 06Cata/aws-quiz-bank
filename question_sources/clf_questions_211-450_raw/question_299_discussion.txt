Which AWS Cloud benefit gives a company the ability to quickly deploy cloud resources to access compute, storage, and database
infrastructures in a matter of minutes?
A. Elasticity
B. Cost savings
C. Agility Most Voted
D. Reliability

Selected Answer: C
A: Elasticity – Refers to automatically scaling resources up or down based on demand, not the speed of initial deployment.
B: Cost savings – Refers to reduced capital expenditure and pay-as-you-go pricing, not deployment speed.
C: Agility – agility is the AWS Cloud benefit that allows companies to quickly deploy and experiment with resources like compute, storage, and
databases in minutes.
D: Reliability – Refers to the ability of the system to recover from failures and remain available, not to deployment speed.
