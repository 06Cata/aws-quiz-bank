Question #326
A company wants to establish a private network connection between AWS and its corporate network.
Which AWS service or feature will meet this requirement?
A. Amazon Connect
B. Amazon Route 53
C. AWS Direct Connect Most Voted
D. VPC peering

Selected Answer: C
A: Amazon Connect – A cloud-based contact center service, not related to network connectivity.
B: Amazon Route 53 – A DNS service used for domain name resolution, not for creating private network connections.
C: AWS Direct Connect it provides a dedicated, private network connection between your on-premises network and AWS, bypassing the public
internet for improved performance and security.
D: VPC peering – Connects two VPCs privately, but does not connect your corporate network to AWS.

C. AWS Direct Connect

corporate office to AWS, bypassing the public internet.
