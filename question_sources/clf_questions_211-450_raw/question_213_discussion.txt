chalaka 2 years, 2 months ago
Selected Answer: D
D. Spot Instances.
The Amazon EC2 instance pricing model that can provide discounts of up to 90% is the "Spot Instances" pricing model. With Spot Instances, you can
bid for unused EC2 capacity, and if your bid price exceeds the current Spot price, your instances will be terminated. This model allows you to take
advantage of unused capacity at significantly reduced prices compared to On-Demand instances. However, it comes with the caveat that your
instances may be terminated if the Spot price increases above your bid price.
