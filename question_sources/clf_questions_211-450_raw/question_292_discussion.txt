B. Data engineering: This capability focuses on managing data pipelines, data storage, and data processing workflows to enable efficient data

Selected Answer: AC
C. Continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) A. Performance and capacity management
Explanation: In AWS CAF, the Platform perspective focuses on building, deploying, and operating the technology foundation (infrastructure,
applications, and tooling). Typical capabilities include:
CI/CD
Performance and capacity management
Infrastructure as code
Compute, storage, networking, and database platforms
Monitoring and observability
Notes on distractors:
B. Data engineering belongs to the Data perspective.
D. Infrastructure protection belongs to the Security perspective.
E. Change and release management belongs to the Operations perspective.
