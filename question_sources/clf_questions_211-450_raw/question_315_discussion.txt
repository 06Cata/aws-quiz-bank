B. People
The AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (AWS CAF) has several perspectives, and the "People" perspective is focused on workforce transformation,
including attracting and developing a digitally fluent high-performance workforce. This perspective emphasizes the importance of organizational
culture, skills development, and creating a diverse and inclusive workforce with the right mix of technical and non-technical skills.
