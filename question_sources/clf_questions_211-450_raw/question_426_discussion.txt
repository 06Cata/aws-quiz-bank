Selected Answer: B
Amazon CloudFront: It’s a content delivery network (CDN) service that distributes content globally through a network of edge locations. CloudFront
caches and delivers static and dynamic content (including HTML, images, videos, and APIs) from the nearest edge location to the user, reducing
latency significantly. By using CloudFront, the company can serve web content efficiently to users worldwide, improving their experience.
