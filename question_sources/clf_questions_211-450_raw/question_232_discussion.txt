B. Record API calls made to AWS services.
AWS CloudTrail is a service that enables governance, compliance, operational auditing, and risk auditing of your AWS account. It records API calls
made on your account, including who made the call, the services used, the actions performed, and when they occurred. This information is valuable
for security analysis, resource change tracking, and troubleshooting.
