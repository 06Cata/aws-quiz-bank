Selected Answer: A
Spot Instances provide the largest discount compared to On-Demand prices, but they come with the caveat that they can be terminated by AWS if
the capacity is needed by On-Demand or Reserved Instances. Spot Instances are suitable for workloads that can tolerate interruptions and are cost-
effective for applications that have flexible start and end times or that can be distributed across multiple instances.
