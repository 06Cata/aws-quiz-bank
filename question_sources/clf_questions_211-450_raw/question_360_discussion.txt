OOJay 1 year, 12 months ago
C. AWS Outposts
AWS Outposts allows you to deploy AWS-designed infrastructure on-premises, providing a consistent hybrid experience. With AWS Outposts, you
can run compute and storage workloads locally on your premises while seamlessly connecting to the rest of AWS's broad array of services in the
cloud.
In this scenario, if the company wants to keep the data local and on-premises to meet regulatory requirements and maintain low latency between
AWS and on-premises resources, AWS Outposts would be the suitable option. It extends the AWS infrastructure to your on-premises location,
providing a hybrid solution that combines the benefits of both on-premises and cloud resources.
