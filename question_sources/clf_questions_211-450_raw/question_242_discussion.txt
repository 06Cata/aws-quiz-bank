C. Amazon Route 53
Amazon Route 53 is a scalable and highly available Domain Name System (DNS) web service provided by AWS. It is designed to route end-user
requests to endpoints, such as Amazon EC2 instances, AWS Elastic Load Balancers, or other resources. Route 53 provides a reliable and cost-effective
way to ensure that users can access your globally distributed ecommerce platform with low-latency and high availability.
