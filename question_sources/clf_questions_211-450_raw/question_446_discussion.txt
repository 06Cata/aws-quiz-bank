Glacier Instant Retrieval
Storage Cost: $0.004 * 1,000 GB = $4.00 per month
Annual Cost (12 months): $4.00 * 12 = $48.00
Retrieval Cost (per retrieval): $0.03 * 1,000 GB = $30.00
Intelligent-Tiering (Archive Access Tier)
Storage Cost: $0.004 * 1,000 GB = $4.00 per month
Annual Cost (12 months): $4.00 * 12 = $48.00
Retrieval Cost (per retrieval): $0.01 * 1,000 GB = $10.00
Request Cost: $10.00 per 1,000 requests
Total Cost for 1 Year (including retrievals):
Glacier Instant Retrieval: $48.00 (storage) + $60.00 (two retrievals) = $108.00
Intelligent-Tiering (Archive Access Tier): $48.00 (storage) + $20.00 (two retrievals) + $20.00 (two requests) = $88.00
