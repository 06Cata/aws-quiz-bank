Selected Answer: D
Loosely coupling components is a design principle that emphasizes minimizing dependencies between different components in a system. This helps
isolate failures, meaning that if one component fails or undergoes changes, it doesn't have a significant impact on other components. This design
principle contributes to increased resilience, flexibility, and easier maintenance of the overall system.
