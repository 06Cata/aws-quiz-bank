Selected Answer: D
When a company uses AWS Regions to increase application availability to users in different countries, it is leveraging the global reach of cloud
computing. AWS Regions are geographically distributed data center clusters, and each Region consists of multiple Availability Zones. Deploying
applications across multiple AWS Regions allows companies to provide low-latency access to their services for users in different geographical
locations, thereby improving global reach and availability.
