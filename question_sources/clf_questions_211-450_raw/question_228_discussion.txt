AWS Compute Optimizer analyzes the utilization metrics of your Amazon EC2 instances and provides recommendations for rightsizing them to
improve performance and reduce costs. By suggesting the optimal instance types and sizes based on historical usage patterns, Compute Optimizer
helps you ensure that your resources are appropriately provisioned to meet your workload requirements without overprovisioning or
underprovisioning.
