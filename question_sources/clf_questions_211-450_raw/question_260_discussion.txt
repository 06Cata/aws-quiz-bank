Selected Answer: B
AWS Organizations is the service that enables you to consolidate multiple AWS accounts into an organization that you create and centrally manage.
It helps you to simplify billing by allowing you to consolidate payment methods for all linked accounts. This way, you can benefit from volume
discounts and have a centralized view of your AWS spending. It also provides features for policy-based management, which helps you enforce
policies across your accounts, among other organizational benefits.
