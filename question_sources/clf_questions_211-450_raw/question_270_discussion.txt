The AWS Snow Family is designed for situations where you need to transfer large amounts of data to and from the AWS Cloud but have limited or no
internet connectivity. In this scenario, AWS Snow Family devices (such as AWS Snowball or AWS Snowmobile) can be used to physically transfer data.
