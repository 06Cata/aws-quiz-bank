Which AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (AWS CAF) capabilities belong to the governance perspective? (Choose two.)
A. Program and project management Most Voted
B. Product management
C. Portfolio management
D. Risk management Most Voted
E. Event management

dragossky 5 months ago
Selected Answer: CD
In the AWS CAF Governance perspective, the standard capabilities are:
Strategy management
Portfolio management
Investment and budgeting
Risk management
Policy and compliance
“Program and project management” is part of the AWS CAF Operations perspective (it sits alongside capabilities like service management, change
management, and incident/event management). “Event management” is also an Operations capability. “Product management” aligns more with the
Business perspective.
Therefore, for the Governance perspective, the correct options from the list are:
C. Portfolio management
D. Risk management
