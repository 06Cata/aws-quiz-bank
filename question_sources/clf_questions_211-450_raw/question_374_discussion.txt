AWS Compute Optimizer analyzes metrics from the past 14 days to generate recommendations for Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2) and EC2
Auto-Scaling Groups, but you can change the settings to 32 or 93 days. Compute Optimizer analyzes metrics from the past 14 days to generate
recommendations for other resource types.

performance and cost efficiency.
