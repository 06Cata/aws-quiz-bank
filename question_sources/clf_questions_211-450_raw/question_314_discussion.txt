Aghajee 1 year, 11 months ago
D. Amazon CloudWatch alarm
To automatically add or remove EC2 instances based on CPU utilization, you can use Amazon CloudWatch alarms with Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling. You
can create a CloudWatch alarm that monitors CPU utilization, and when the alarm threshold is breached, it triggers an Auto Scaling action to either
add or remove EC2 instances, depending on your configuration.
Therefore, the correct answer is D. Amazon CloudWatch alarm.
