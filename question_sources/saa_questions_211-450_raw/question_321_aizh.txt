Question #321
What should a solutions architect do to ensure that all objects uploaded to an Amazon S3 bucket are encrypted?
解决方案架构师应如何确保上传到 Amazon S3 存储桶的所有对象迗已加密？
A. Update the bucket policy to deny if the PutObject does not have an s3:x-amz-acl header set.
更新存储桶策略以拒绝 PutObject 请求，如果未设置 s3:x-amz-acl 标头。
B. Update the bucket policy to deny if the PutObject does not have an s3:x-amz-acl header set to private.
更新存储桶策略以拒绝 PutObject 请求，如果 s3:x-amz-acl 标头未设置为 private。
C. Update the bucket policy to deny if the PutObject does not have an aws:SecureTransport header set to true.
更新存储桶策略以拒绝 PutObject 请求，如果 aws:SecureTransport 标头未设置为 true。

2. 选项分析
A. Update the bucket policy to deny if the PutObject does not have an s3:x-amz-acl header set.
迾误：s3:x-amz-acl标头用于设置对象的访輐控制列表（ACL），与加密无关。
