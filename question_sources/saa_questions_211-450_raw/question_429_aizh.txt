Question #429
The following IAM policy is attached to an IAM group. This is the only policy applied to the group.
What are the effective IAM permissions of this policy for group members?
以下 IAM 策略已附加到一个 IAM 组。这是唯一应用于该组的策略。
此策略对组成员的有效 IAM 权限是什么？
A. Group members are permitted any Amazon EC2 action within the us-east-1 Region. Statements after the Allow permission
are not applied.
组成员被允许在 us-east-1 区域内执行任何 Amazon EC2 操作。允许权限之后的语句不适用。
B. Group members are denied any Amazon EC2 permissions in the us-east-1 Region unless they are logged in with multi-factor
authentication (MFA).
组成员被拒绝在 us-east-1 区域内执行任何 Amazon EC2 权限，除非他们使用多因素身份验证 (MFA) 登录。
C. Group members are allowed the ec2:StopInstances and ec2:TerminateInstances permissions for all Regions when logged in
with multi-factor authentication (MFA). Group members are permitted any other Amazon EC2 action.
组成员被允许在所有区域内执行 ec2:StopInstances 和 ec2:TerminateInstances 权限，但前提是他们使用多因素身份验证 (MFA)
登录。组成员被允许执行任何其他 Amazon EC2 操作。
D. Group members are allowed the ec2:StopInstances and ec2:TerminateInstances permissions for the us-east-1 Region only
when logged in with multi-factor authentication (MFA). Group members are permitted any other Amazon EC2 action within the
us-east-1 Region. Most Voted
组成员仅在使用多因素身份验证 (MFA) 登录时被允许在 us-east-1 区域内执行 ec2:StopInstances 和 ec2:TerminateInstances 权
限。组成员被允许在 us-east-1 区域内执行任何其他 Amazon EC2 操作。
题目分析与解答
1. 考察的知识点
IAM策略的优先级和评估顺序。
IAM策略中的条件语句（Condition）的使用，包括 StringEquals 和 BoolIfExists。
允许（Allow）和拒绝（Deny）权限的交互。
多因素身份验证（MFA）的条件控制。
2. 选项分析
A. Group members are permitted any Amazon EC2 action within the us-east-1 Region. Statements after the Allow
permission are not applied.
错误：IAM策略的评估顺序是所有语句都会被评估，且拒绝（Deny）权限优先于允许（Allow）。因此，后续的拒绝语句会影响最
终权限。
B. Group members are denied any Amazon EC2 permissions in the us-east-1 Region unless they are logged in with multi-
factor authentication (MFA).
错误：策略中明确允许了所有 EC2 操作（ec2:*）在 us-east-1 区域内执行，只有特定的操作（ec2:StopInstances 和
ec2:TerminateInstances）在未使用 MFA 时被拒绝。
C. Group members are allowed the ec2:StopInstances and ec2:TerminateInstances permissions for all Regions when logged
in with multi-factor authentication (MFA). Group members are permitted any other Amazon EC2 action.
错误：策略中的允许语句明确限制了操作范围为 us-east-1 区域，因此其他区域的权限不适用。
D. Group members are allowed the ec2:StopInstances and ec2:TerminateInstances permissions for the us-east-1 Region
only when logged in with multi-factor authentication (MFA). Group members are permitted any other Amazon EC2 action
within the us-east-1 Region.
正确：策略的第一条语句允许所有 EC2 操作在 us-east-1 区域内执行。第二条语句拒绝了 ec2:StopInstances 和
ec2:TerminateInstances 操作，除非用户使用了 MFA。因此，最终权限符合描述。
