Selected Answer: B
The answer is B. Align.
The Align phase of the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (AWS CAF) focuses on aligning your organization's strategic goals with your cloud strategy.
This includes:
Identifying capability gaps: Assessing your organization's current capabilities and identifying areas where you need to improve.
Developing a cloud strategy: Creating a roadmap for your cloud adoption journey.
Defining your target operating model: Determining how you will manage and operate your cloud environment.
By aligning your organization's goals with your cloud strategy, you can ensure that your cloud adoption efforts are focused on delivering business
value.

migration. It is more about building organizational consensus than about identifying capability gaps.
